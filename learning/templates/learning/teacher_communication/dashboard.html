📋 How to Use Teacher Communication

🎯 What you can do:
  • Send messages to class teachers and subject teachers
  • Discuss your child's progress and performance
  • Ask questions about homework and assignments
  • Request parent-teacher meetings
  • Get updates on classroom activities
💡 Tips for effective communication:
  • Be specific about your concerns or questions
  • Include your child's name in the subject line
  • Be respectful and professional in your messages
  • Allow 24-48 hours for teacher responses
  • Use appropriate priority levels for urgent matters
